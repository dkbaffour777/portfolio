About

After a few months in a three-year computer science field at Kumasi Technical University in Ghana, I was introduced to front-end development by
a colleague who inspired me about this particular field.
I always wanted to learn more, and the more I researched the more knowledge and skills I acquired. I became a self-taught web developer
by learning and practicing on a daily basis.

I worked at a real estate company in Kumasi, Ghana as a web developer. The technologies I used were HTML/CSS/JavaScript/React/PHP.
I later on moved to the US permanently and after a while I still felt there where some things missing
within my journey to become a professional full stack web developer, searching for the right tuition to accomplish this goal.
Another friend recommended a coding bootcamp at University of Arizona, which I am currently enrolled in and aspiring as a
full stack web developer and it has been a wonderful journey so far.
